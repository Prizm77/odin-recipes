Garlic-Parmesan Shrimp

Delicious crispy shrimp coated in a bread crumb-Parmesan mixture. My family can't get enough of it! I would suggest doubling the recipe if you have very large eaters because this is addictive! Serve with pasta and salad.

Chicken Pot Pie

A delicious chicken pie made from scratch with carrots, peas, and celery in a pre-made crust. Add thyme and poultry seasoning for more flavor.

Mashed Potatoes

If you love good, old-fashioned mashed potatoes, this is the perfect recipe. Made with Idaho potatoes, milk, butter, and optional garlic, this go-to recipe for simple, homemade mashed potatoes is a classic holiday dish. Learn how to make mashed potatoes that come out smooth, creamy, and delicious every time. Serve with gravy or extra butter on top.
